Where Tonebox already wins / ties the leaders

  • Playback depth on par with Plexamp/Symfonium: true gapless plus stream-prefetch, crossfade, ReplayGain, and a 10-band EQ — features Spotify/Apple/Tidal/YT Music mostly lack on desktop.
  • Native dual scrobbling (Last.fm and ListenBrainz) — most mainstream apps need a third-party bridge.
  • Discovery + stats (Home/Mixes/History/Radio) that Navidrome-web and the other open clients simply don't have.
  • Polished macOS UX: hero detail pages, collapsible now-playing bar, floating mini-player, waveform scrubber, multi-select — genuinely nicer than most Subsonic clients.
  • You own the library and it's free — the whole point vs Spotify/Tidal.

Where it lags the field

  • macOS only — every leader is cross-platform; no phone = no listening away from the desk. Biggest strategic gap.
  • Casting limited to AirPlay — Plexamp/Symfonium/Spotify reach Chromecast, Sonos, and speaker groups.
  • No podcasts or internet radio — Subsonic supports both; the open clients expose them.
  • Single server / no account switching.
  • No self-contained app identity yet (it lives inside Tonebox): no onboarding, updates, or downloads-manager of its own.

Suggested roadmap — biggest impact first

1
iOS / iPadOS companion large
The one gap shared with every leader. Reuse the Subsonic client + stores; a phone app (even read-only-ish) turns this from “a nice desktop player” into a real ecosystem. Offline sync of downloads is the killer piece.
2
Casting beyond AirPlay — Chromecast / Sonos / speaker groups medium
Closes the gap with Plexamp/Symfonium for whole-home audio. Chromecast + UPnP/DLNA cover most speakers.
3
Podcasts & internet radio smaller
Cheap parity win — the Subsonic API already exposes both (getPodcasts, getInternetRadioStations). Mostly UI + a couple of client calls.
4
Downloads manager + offline mode medium
A real screen: what's downloaded, storage used, evict, auto-download playlists, and a global “offline” toggle. Table-stakes for a standalone player.
5
Multi-server / account switching smaller
Store N connections, switch quickly. Natural once NavidromeConfig is decoupled for the standalone extraction.
6
Sonic-analysis / mood mixes large
Plexamp's real differentiator. Analyze downloaded audio (tempo/energy/key) to build “more like this” + smart transitions — where Tonebox could beat the open clients, not just match them.
7
Parametric EQ + crossfeed / DSP medium
Upgrade the 10-band graphic EQ toward Plexamp/Roon territory: parametric bands, presets per-output, headphone crossfeed. The EQ tap engine already exists.
