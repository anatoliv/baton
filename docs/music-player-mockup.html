Tonebox Player — redesign mockup

A concrete look at the proposed direction: a color-from-artwork full-screen player, richer browse, an artist hero page, and synced lyrics — the patterns that make Plexamp, Apple Music, and Tidal feel premium. These are static HTML mocks (simulated album art via CSS) to convey layout & feel, not final pixels.

1 · Full-screen “Now Playing” hero headline change

The whole background is derived from the album art’s dominant colors (Plexamp “UltraBlur” / Apple Music). Large art, big type, thin scrubber, like + 5-star, and an “Up Next” queue you can reorder. Expands out of the mini-player.

6 · Existing solutions to look at (and what to borrow)

Open these to see the real thing — each nails a specific pattern above.

Plexamp

The self-hosted gold standard. “UltraBlur” paints the whole UI from album-art colors; stunning full-screen player + visualizers.

Borrow → the adaptive color backdrop (screen 1) and full-screen player.

medium.com/plexlabs/plexamp-v3
plex.tv/plexamp

Apple Music (macOS)

The typography, motion album art, color-adaptive full-screen player and karaoke synced lyrics reference.

Borrow → lyrics highlight (screen 4), type scale, full-screen gradient.

SwiftUI recreation of Apple Music animations

Doppler (macOS)

Best-designed local-library Mac app — album/artist-first, uncluttered, fast, native-feeling. The restraint benchmark.

Borrow → density, artist/album-first nav, native polish.

brushedtype.co/doppler/features

Cassette (macOS/iOS)

A native SwiftUI Subsonic client that looks Apple-grade — same platform & backend as Tonebox, so the most directly copyable target.

Borrow → proof a native Subsonic client can look premium.

getcassette.app

Tidal

Full-bleed artwork as the hero of every screen; color-adaptive headers; credits.

Borrow → full-bleed blurred backdrops behind pages (screens 3–4).

tidal vs qobuz (UI comparison)

Feishin (desktop, free)

Richest-looking desktop self-hosted client today (Spotify-like, full-screen player, themes). Good desktop-layout reference.

Borrow → desktop layout model + full-screen player.

github.com/jeffvli/feishin
